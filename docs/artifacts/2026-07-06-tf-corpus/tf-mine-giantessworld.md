## 1. "I want to be a GIANTESS" — Tizio42
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=16524 (ch.1-2)

- **Trigger.** A short bullied girl, Clara (~110 cm), forms the belief that eating large
  quantities will make her grow ("I just need to eat more to grow more"). Her brother
  Alessio encourages the big portions. Chapter 1 ends with a seeded psychological hook: she
  murmurs "Big... bigger... biggest" in her sleep and the narration suggests "something in
  her brain had been unlocked" — a mental trigger preceding any physical change.
- **Sequence.** Setup chapter shows no change (measured/marked on the bathroom wall, holds
  at 110 cm). In the growth chapter the change is tracked by external markers rather than
  interior sensation: (1) week 1 — shoes tight at the toes, first re-measure 110→116 cm;
  (2) weeks 2-8 — shirts and pants progressively snug, Christmas shoes don't fit by January,
  feet outgrow new winter boots, visibly taller than classmates; (3) end — formal re-measure
  116→126 cm, i.e. 10 cm over two months. A shirt tears at the seams and sleeves.
- **Mind.** Confidence rises with height; bullying lands less as she becomes bigger and more
  visible. No loss of self.
- **Pace.** Gradual over weeks/months but flagged as abnormally fast ("10 cm in just two
  months").
- **Reversibility.** Not addressed; reads as permanent.
- **Notable specifics.** Growth is entirely bodily-external here — clothing/shoe fit and
  wall-mark measurements are the whole vocabulary of change; almost no proprioceptive
  interior. Foot growth is tracked separately from height. Framed as diet-driven but the
  "unlocked brain" hint implies wish/psychic causation underneath.

## 2. "Rising Higher, Falling Lower" — JFoxxo
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=16632 (ch.1-2)

- **Trigger.** Ms. Akari Sakamoto covertly doses Joshua's wine — empties "a small, clear
  vial" of an "enhanced formula" into his glass while he's away — a supplement pitched as
  making women "smarter, stronger, taller… better." She takes it herself. Non-consensual for
  him. This is a paired, opposite transformation: he shrinks, she grows, from the same agent.
- **Sequence (Joshua, shrinking).** (1) Hands look "smaller wrapped around" the glass, the
  stem "thicker between his fingers"; (2) floor "felt closer than it should," balance
  unsteady "as if his legs had shortened"; (3) suit jacket hangs looser, shoulders droop,
  cuffs slide over the backs of his hands, shoes loose with feet sliding inside; (4) voice
  comes out "softer... Higher, almost"; (5) by ch.2 he's lost ~half a foot from a 6'0"
  baseline, trunks fall loose and need tightening.
- **Sequence (Akari, growing).** (1) Thighs press against her suit pants, "fuller than they
  had been"; (2) blouse pulls tight across the chest, a top button strains; (3) shoulders
  fill the jacket, seams taut; (4) hips roll and "swell," pants hugging new curves; (5) by
  ch.2 breasts "much bigger," threatening to tear her swimsuit top, hips broad, thighs
  "nearly as thick around as his entire torso," plus muscular development — "ridges of
  muscle," corded arms and shoulders, "hard and large." Reads taller and "more commanding."
- **Mind.** He drifts toward submission/acceptance, feeling "small," "reduced," shame mixed
  with arousal, shifting to compliance. No cognitive change stated for her beyond confidence.
- **Pace.** Gradual across an evening, then the bulk within ~24 hours. He first blames alcohol
  and lighting before conceding it's real.
- **Reversibility.** Not addressed; reads permanent.
- **Notable specifics.** Two-body zero-sum feel (his mass appears to migrate to her). The
  early beats are perceptual-ambiguous — object-relative cues (glass stem thicker, floor
  closer) that could pass as drunkenness. Voice pitch shift included as a change axis.

## 3. "The human race at the mercy of two teenage girls" — Giantess3D
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=16658 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Two girls (Natalie, Claire) touched a fallen alien artifact at age 13; it
  "exploded in their hands," granting permanent, consciously-controlled size-changing plus
  invulnerability.
- **Sequence.** Fully volitional: they decide to grow, "began to grow," "kept growing,"
  chose when to stop. No stepwise bodily beats — it's smooth, willed expansion. They exceed
  prior personal ceilings (Natalie's old max 50 ft, Claire's 300 ft).
- **Mind.** Full awareness throughout; emotional arc of wonder → exhilaration → recognition
  of power. No loss of self.
- **Pace.** Gradual but on-demand and controllable; start and stop are chosen.
- **Reversibility.** Freely reversible (an ability, can grow and presumably shrink back).
- **Notable specifics.** Clothing grows with them and becomes invulnerable too ("any clothing
  we are wearing becomes invulnerable") — so no clothes-tearing consequence at all, the
  opposite convention from most growth stories. Growth here has no downside/vulnerability;
  it's clean power fantasy.

## 4. "A Rose By Any Other Name..." — nancyarcher
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=2497 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Unspecified in this chapter; associated with her time at the Beast's castle.
  Beauty returns home already transformed — the change happened off-page during her absence.
- **Sequence.** Presented as accomplished fact, not shown in progress: she now stands taller
  than a two-story barn, "the size of the two-story thatch roofed house." Immediate spatial
  consequence — she can "scarcely fit" the house she grew up in; her brothers don't recognize
  her.
- **Mind.** No psychological alteration indicated.
- **Pace.** Unknown (off-page); could be gradual or instant.
- **Reversibility.** Unaddressed; the Beast's line "You are not the same" hints at permanence.
- **Notable specifics.** A "discovered after the fact" growth — the emotional beat is
  homecoming-at-wrong-scale (can't fit the childhood home, family estrangement by size)
  rather than the mechanics of growing.

## 5. "Comet" — banfield
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=934 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** An alien invasion masked as a comet; female aliens from a dying world are
  implanted into human women, who then convert/consume human men. Victim shown: Tony, via
  the infected Rebecca.
- **Sequence (shrink-to-absorption).** (1) Psychological onset — uncontrollable attraction
  and sexual compulsion toward the infected woman; (2) gradual shrinking over hours — by
  evening he's "about the size of a lad of maybe 8 or 9," feet reaching just short of her
  knees; (3) overnight, further down to "no larger than a new born babe"; (4) consumed
  entirely into her body, physical form gone.
- **Mind.** Consciousness persists through absorption; his essence is to be "reborn" in her
  male offspring, retaining memories of his own conquest/defeat.
- **Pace.** Gradual over ~24 hours, driven by repeated sexual consumption.
- **Reversibility.** Non-reversible; the essence integrates permanently.
- **Notable specifics.** Shrinking here is calibrated to human-age landmarks (8-9 year old
  → newborn) rather than inches/feet. Affects many men across the city simultaneously
  (mass event), only Tony's thread detailed. Transformation is a reproductive/absorption
  mechanism, not a size gag — endpoint is becoming genetic material.

## 6. "Growth Dreams" — Felix Knotrock
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=2491 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** A homemade laptop-like device that programs size changes precisely — the user
  specifies which body part, target size, and duration ("what part of her body I want to
  grow, how big and in how much time").
- **Sequence.** Experimental and targeted: narrator self-tests, enlarging from 9 in → 24 in
  → 8 ft in length; then applies localized growth to Sherri's breasts, DD → "MM cup" over
  twenty minutes. Her XL t-shirt "got tighter and tighter until" it shredded.
- **Mind.** Heightened sensitivity, addiction-like craving for the transformations; no
  permanent cognitive change.
- **Pace.** Deliberately gradual and timed (the 20-minute breast expansion); narrator asserts
  slow change yields better sensation than instant.
- **Reversibility.** Explicitly temporary and reversible — once Sherri learns it's reversible
  she enjoys it; they cycle through many transformations nightly for a week (cyclic use).
- **Notable specifics.** Fine-grained authored control (part, magnitude, rate as separate
  dials). The device produces "aesthetically enhanced" results, not just scaling. Cyclic/
  repeatable is the defining trait — transformation as a nightly recreational loop.

## 7. "America Runs on Hanna" — Jaime Hillborne
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=2481 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Instant and unexplained — Hanna simply finds herself transformed while sitting
  in her car at school. No mechanism given.
- **Sequence (person → coffee).** (1) Wakes "in a dark place" with "no control over her arms
  and legs," then realizes those limbs "did not, in fact, still exist"; (2) loses mouth and
  physical form entirely; (3) realizes she has become "Bella's coffee" — a liquid in a cup.
- **Mind.** Full sentience preserved as liquid — "could still feel, but she had no control
  over her movements." Awareness persists through being drunk, swallowed, digested, excreted;
  some of her consciousness "separates" during the process. Emotional arc: terror →
  resignation → worshipping Bella as a "goddess."
- **Pace.** The form-change is instant; the consumption is gradual, "sip by sip."
- **Reversibility.** Not reversible — becomes a permanent recurring state; "Every cup Bella
  has had since then has contained the pearl of Hanna's consciousness."
- **Notable specifics.** Transformation into a formless consumable, not a smaller body — total
  loss of body/limbs/mouth but retained interior sentience. Consciousness fragments/persists
  across many cups (distributed, ongoing existence as a drink).

## 8. "as amys gum" — thestageofrage
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=2146 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Spoken wish — narrator confesses wanting to be "shrunk and used as a piece of
  gum"; Amy answers "so be it" and he blacks out. Change happens during unconsciousness.
- **Sequence (person → gum).** Wakes already micro-sized in "a very dark room," limbs
  hemmed by fabric (inside her mouth). No gradual shrink shown. Then the material change:
  under her teeth his body "seemed to stretch and wrap around her teeth like I too was a
  piece of gum," becoming "wet and malleable" and merging with actual gum into one substance.
- **Mind.** Fully conscious and sensate throughout; obedient — licks her foot on instruction.
- **Pace.** Shrink is instant/off-page; the gum-merge is experienced live under chewing.
- **Reversibility.** Reversible — Amy restores him to his "non-chewy form" while he sleeps.
- **Notable specifics.** Two-stage: first shrink, then a substance/property change (rigid
  body → stretchy malleable gum) that fuses with existing gum. Sensation of being chewed
  rendered as "a small and quite pleasant pressure."

## 9. "Footified" — asukafan2001
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=439 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Physical contact — Kelly forces her foot onto Dave's chest, pressing the air
  out of him; his body "jolted and shook like he was having a seizure," then he blacks out.
- **Sequence (person → part of a foot).** Narrative-gap: he awakens already merged, his face
  "spread across the bottom of her foot," integrated into the sole.
- **Mind.** Retains full awareness and memory; can perceive surroundings; voice reduced to
  "only but a whisper."
- **Pace.** Instant (blackout to already-merged).
- **Reversibility.** No reversal indicated; Kelly frames it as permanent — his existence now
  "anything more then just the bottom of my foot" denied.
- **Notable specifics.** Becomes a functional body-part of another person (living skin of a
  sole), not an object or a smaller body. Proprioception rewritten to the foot's frame — he
  feels the foot's movement, pressure, friction, weight, smell as Kelly walks.

## 10. "Glitter" — UnanimousDecision
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=2204 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Unexplained; Scott naps in a back room and wakes transformed.
- **Sequence (person → glitter speck).** (1) Wakes unable to move or find his limbs;
  (2) discovers "his hands weren't moveable and in fact weren't even there"; (3) realizes he's
  become a speck of dark glitter on a banquet-hall floor only when Shannon steps on him.
- **Mind.** Full consciousness, no ability to move or communicate ("I'm down here!" yelled
  only in his mind). Emotional arc: confusion → horror → rage ("I'm GLITTER?!") → despair.
- **Pace.** Instant/off-page during sleep.
- **Reversibility.** None indicated; fate left open and grim.
- **Notable specifics.** Endpoint is an inert decorative particle — smaller and more helpless
  than "micro"; indistinguishable from surrounding glitter, so identity is lost to observers.
  Sensory register is entirely passive/received: crushing underfoot, then confined in a shoe
  amid "sweat drops, toe junk, dirt" and foul odor. Total loss of agency with retained
  suffering awareness.

## 11. "A Difference in Opinion" — neverstill14
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=1099 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Lauren gives Sean a spiked drink without his knowledge; he downs it to avoid
  offending her, then passes out.
- **Sequence.** Result only, no process — wakes in her room, naked, reduced to ~1 inch tall;
  Lauren's foot reads ~9 inches to him.
- **Mind.** Full consciousness and memory retained.
- **Pace.** Instant, while unconscious.
- **Reversibility.** Not addressed in ch.1.
- **Notable specifics.** The potion adds torture-conditions beyond size: permanent pain
  sensitivity without death, hunger without need to eat, suppression of normal bodily
  functions — the transformation is deliberately weaponized into indefinite suffering rather
  than a clean shrink. (Category tags list breast enlargement, but none occurs in ch.1.)

## 12. "The Giant Escort" — Nugget01
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=16800 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Mark's shrink-ray device with an auto-timer return-beam; a willing test.
- **Sequence.** The bodily shrink is under-described; what's rendered is the sensation of the
  beam and the environmental aftermath — "a warm compression, like being squeezed through a
  keyhole made of static," then the world expanding around him rather than a felt shrinking of
  the body.
- **Mind.** No cognitive effect noted.
- **Pace.** Unclear whether instant or gradual; the process itself is elided.
- **Reversibility.** Fully reversible and repeatable — the auto return-beam restores him to
  1.73 m / ~5'8".
- **Notable specifics.** Scale conveyed purely through environmental metaphor after the
  fact: garage → "a stadium," furniture → architecture, workbench → "a concrete overpass."
  Clothing/balance/proprioception during the change are not addressed. Consent + safe +
  repeatable framing (an escort service premise).

## 13. "[Commission] Fairysitter" — jellytea819
URL: https://www.giantessworld.net/viewstory.php?sid=16692 (ch.1)

- **Trigger.** Accidental fairy-dust inhalation — Ari (a fairy) scatters dust in a playful
  scuffle, Touma inhales it while coughing. Untested on a human before, so potency is a
  surprise. Notably an *ongoing* (not one-shot) shrink.
- **Sequence.** Continuous reduction, 180 cm → 16.5 cm across the chapter, shown mid-process
  rather than as a jump: mid-embrace Ari notes "My palm can cover most of your head already."
  As he passes ~16 cm he ends up "staring straight at Ari's perky breasts" (perspective
  landmark). Environment rescales — his TV looms "larger than what the university lecture hall
  projectors could display," the sofa cushion becomes ground-terrain.
- **Mind.** No cognitive change; disorientation from shifting perspective.
- **Pace.** Gradual and continuous throughout the scene, still shrinking as they interact.
- **Reversibility.** Effectively permanent for now — Ari can't undo it ("I don't think I
  remember how to undo fairy dust shrinking").
- **Notable specifics.** Explicit strength-differential consequence of proportional shrink —
  at matched height his limbs are "much thinner than Ari's," so she's physically stronger and
  can lift him effortlessly; scale change carries a power/strength change, not just size.
  Landmarks are relational (her palm over his head, eye-level to her breasts) rather than
  measured.
