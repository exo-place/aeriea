## 1. "Gloves" (author: uncredited on page) — inanimate/pool-toy
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/Gloves

- **Trigger:** Putting on a pair of custom vinyl "deer" gloves — double-walled inflatable vinyl with air valves.
- **Sequence:**
  1. Gloves enlarge and *melt/flow* up the arms, vanishing under the shirt sleeves (hands/arms first).
  2. Head/face: a puffy antler pushes out of the skull; the right half of the face converts to vinyl; the eye is replaced by a flat "big painted cartoon eye"; the ear becomes an air-filled pocket; nose/lips/mouth fill with vinyl and the face "poofs out" into a rounded muzzle.
  3. Torso: vinyl spreads under the clothes; body swells into a rounded balloon shape; a large inflation valve forms where the navel was.
  4. Limbs lock into a fixed hugging pose that springs back when moved (still movable, but sprung); an inflated tail balloons out behind.
  5. Feet swell until they rupture the shoes.
- **Novel anatomy:** Seams land exactly where a real inflatable would have them; forearm valves retained; a product tag appears reading "Inflatable Deer Pool Toy" with deflation instructions.
- **Mind:** Human awareness and horror kept fully intact throughout.
- **Pace:** Gradual, continuous.
- **Reversibility:** Implied deflatable via the belly valve.
- **Distinctive:** Body becomes a labeled commercial product; posing spring-back; cartoon painted-on eye replacing a real one.

## 2. "An Ovidian Metamorphosis; Poseidon's Gift" (author: uncredited) — equine (mare), verse
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/An_Ovidian_Metamorphosis;_Poseidon%E2%80%99s_Gift

- **Subject/trigger:** Aglea, a priestess of Demeter, begs the gods to reshape her into a mare to win Hippolytus (who is cursed to reject mortal women).
- **Sequence (ordered):**
  1. Voice goes first — speech alternates between words and neighs until only neighs remain.
  2. Skeleton: spine lengthens, a tail grows from the back.
  3. Extremities: hands and feet sculpt into horn-clad hooves; fingers merge into a single toe.
  4. Posture: back and neck arch into a powerful curve; limbs grow long and thin.
  5. Musculature blossoms in rump and chest.
  6. Mane sprouts; breasts shrink and reposition; tail grows hair.
  7. Face changes *last* — into a long graceful muzzle with velvet lips.
- **Mind:** Alters during the change — "wild, yet wondrous" galloping-thoughts intrude mid-transformation; afterward her mind becomes a mare's and she forgets her human love.
- **Pace:** Gradual, involuntary, accompanied by agony (pain worsened by resistance).
- **Reversibility:** Permanent.
- **Distinctive:** Voice-loss leads the sequence; face is the very last thing to go; mind-erasure of the original motive (the love that caused it).

- **Distinctive:** Tack (bridle/bit) fused into living flesh; the horror is the mute retained human mind under labor and the whip.

## 4. "Butterflies are the Gentlest" (author: uncredited) — mass event / plant-pollination reproductive TF
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/Butterflies_are_the_Gentlest

- **Trigger:** A simultaneous worldwide event around noon (during Laura & Robert's wedding); brief universal nausea marks onset.
- **Primary change (varies by geographic zone!):**
  - Athens, Georgia zone: women lose the clitoris and *all* internal reproductive organs (ovaries, uterus, tubes) but keep outward appearance; men lose penis, testicles, and prostate and gain a crude pseudo-vaginal structure described as a child's naive idea of female anatomy — no functional internal reproductive tissue.
  - Other zones got entirely different bodies: four-armed people in D.C.; marsupial-type people with pouches and extra eyes in Bowling Green, KY.
- **Secondary change (weeks later, Florida Everglades):** Hot humid conditions trigger large white flowers (two layers of five petals) to grow from the pseudo-vaginal region, with working pistils and stamens; tissue hypersensitive/painful; arousal redirected toward the environment. Pollinators (hummingbirds, bees, butterflies) pollinate the people, causing conception.
- **Tertiary change:** After petals drop, ultrasound reveals a newly grown "undersized but roughly human-looking womb" in each person; viable pregnancy follows despite the earlier total organ loss.
- **Mind:** Main couple's cognition mostly unchanged; a separate zone's character (Anne Hartz) undergoes neurological change altering attraction, bonding, and social priorities — so some zones changed the brain too.
- **Pace:** Instant for the initial organ loss; gradual/environmental for the flowering.
- **Reversibility:** Unknown; characters hope it may reverse as suddenly as it came.
- **Distinctive:** Reproduction offloaded to literal botanical pollination by insects/birds; geographically-quantized outcomes (different body per city); organ deletion then regrowth into a novel path.

URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/On_Display

- **Pace:** Fast onset.
- **Reversibility:** Irreversible within the story's span.

## 7. "Scales" (author: Clicketyclack) — dragon (feral quadruped)
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/User:Clicketyclack/Scales

- **Trigger:** Begins with just "a slight pinch"; cause otherwise unstated.
- **Sequence (ordered):**
  1. Skin along the arm raises and thickens into rigid inflexible discs.
  2. Hands: fingers flatten, fingerprints harden, fingernails give way to claws.
  3. Upper body: bony protrusions and spikes push out along arms and shoulder-blades.
  4. Torso: ribs expand outward; skin stretches and tears away to reveal smooth scales beneath; breasts vanish (so female->neuter/male-ish).
  5. Lower body: pelvis rotates for quadrupedal stance; legs splay outward; feet elongate with a lengthened big toe.
  6. Spine: vertebrae push up along an elongating neck and a growing tail.
  7. Head last: cheekbones widen, jaw extends, teeth fall out, tongue lengthens, ears vanish, a snout forms.
- **Mind:** Present throughout — continuous internal monologue; ends with the narrator swearing they've become "a fucking dragon."
- **Pace:** Rapid, continuous, painful; sensory loss along the way.
- **Reversibility:** No indication of reversal.
- **Distinctive:** Skin *tears off* to expose scales already underneath; whole-limb spike growth; ends as a feral quadruped, not an anthro.

## 8. "So You're Becoming a Taur" (author: uncredited) — taur (nanite pandemic), guide format
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/So_You%27re_Becoming_a_Taur

- **Trigger:** Exposure to GeneTech's "TaurMe" nanites — voluntary dose or accidental (airports, water supply, environmental contamination). Not person-to-person contagious.
- **Sequence (staged over days):**
  1. Hours 0-24: mild fever; fur begins growing at the *midsection* first; animal-specific markings appear (leopard spots / skunk stripes / raccoon rings) so species is usually readable within a day.
  2. ~Hour 24: spine extends into a tail — starts as a "weird fleshy" nub before it fills with fur.
  3. Days 2-7 (usually 2-3 days): the taur lower body forms via one of two variants:
     - **Midpaw:** brand-new animal feet sprout from the torso; the torso extends and the spine bends into an L to become the second (horizontal) body.
     - **Hindpaw:** the original feet stay; the body extends backward and grows new rear legs.
- **Novel anatomy:** A *second* heart and a dual-lung system in the added torso; optional bolt-ons in some strains — "USB data ports, skeletal reinforcement"; some forepaws grow limited opposable thumbs; some species get hooves on hands/feet; boosted endurance/speed/climbing.
- **Mind:** Several days of vivid dreams of four-legged movement and animal behavior that ease psychological adjustment to the new body; gender change frequently rides along with the physical change.
- **Pace:** Days; awkward, painful intermediate stage where rest/assistance is advised.
- **Reversibility:** Effectively irreversible — nanites deactivate once done; only a different TaurMe strain can reverse it.
- **Distinctive:** Extra internal organs to support the second torso; the two anatomical "variants" (grow-new-feet vs keep-old-feet); infrastructure-vector pandemic framing.

## 9. "Nothing Up My Sleeve" (author: uncredited) — inanimate (living fursuit)
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/Nothing_Up_My_Sleeve

- **Subject/trigger:** Evan, a software engineer, is not changed by any external agent — extended imaginative roleplay in a partial fox fursuit slowly makes him *become* the suit.
- **Sequence (very gradual, discovered piecemeal):**
  1. A velcro strap starts feeling "a little itchy"; the partial suit (head, paws, tail) feels more natural each day.
  2. At a BBQ he's wearing the suit without remembering putting it on; he eats a burger and tastes it normally — the suit's mouth is now his mouth.
  3. In a mirror: the costume's eyes and his own are one and the same; the partial suit has silently become a full suit.
  4. He pulls off his left paw and finds *empty space* where his arm should be; the detached paw, held in his other hand, still clenches on command.
  5. His head detaches entirely and stays conscious and mobile.
- **Novel anatomy:** No internal organs at all — just athletic mesh inside, "no insides whatsoever"; needs stuffing to keep from collapsing under gravity; digits with claws still type; can eat and drink despite being fabric.
- **Mind:** Unchanged — thoughts and feelings stay normal; he watches each impossibility with calm curiosity, no panic.
- **Pace:** Slow, creeping, self-driven by the act of pretending.
- **Reversibility:** Explicitly permanent; ends accepted by coworkers as an animate costume.
- **Distinctive:** Detachable still-functioning parts; hollow mesh body needing stuffing; transformation powered by roleplay rather than magic or tech.

## 13. "Not Beyond Conjecture" (author: uncredited) — mermaid/siren (dolphin-tailed), reversible
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/Not_Beyond_Conjecture

- **Trigger:** Wizard Kasrigan curses the (male) protagonist mid-drowning after he's thrown overboard; the change proceeds underwater.
- **Sequence (ordered):**
  1. Gills open first — rhythmic slit-grooves along the neck (survival breathing before anything else).
  2. Teeth sharpen and multiply — a second row grows in behind the first.
  3. Groin fuses — penis and testicles partly absorb into the thighs as the thighs grow together.
  4. Webbing grows between the fingers and between the upper arms and the ribs.
  5. Legs finish fusing into a slender dolphin lower body with a horizontal fluke; toes fuse/reabsorb.
  6. On surfacing: small breasts have formed (so also M->F).
- **Novel anatomy:** Horizontal (dolphin/cetacean) fluke rather than vertical fish tail; second row of teeth; involuntary compulsion to sing (siren).
- **Mind:** Fully conscious through both directions of the change.
- **Pace:** Fairly quick onset underwater.
- **Reversibility:** Reverses over ~3 days in reverse-ish order (gills close, fluke splits back to legs/feet, webbing recedes, back tooth-row reabsorbs) — but imperfectly: keeps siren-sharp teeth, breast tissue, and altered face, ending phenotypically female.
- **Distinctive:** Rare *reversible* case, and the reversal is incomplete — a permanent residue (teeth, breasts, face) is left behind.

## 14. "Iskin" (author: GreyDragon) — human->AI-integrated cyborg (mind-first)
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/User:GreyDragon/Iskin

- **Subject/trigger:** A disabled man (cerebral palsy, brain damage) is kidnapped by slavers and surgically fused to an "iskin" — an 8th-gen artificial-consciousness cluster — as a control interface.
- **Sequence:**
  1. Slavers implant a dense field of micro I/O ports across his shaved skull, hardwiring his brain to the AI cluster (mind changes *first*: his consciousness immediately splits into part-human, part-AI-collective thinking).
  2. Control protocols fail during an attack, leaving him autonomous as a dual human-brain + AI entity.
  3. Later, voluntary body work: rejuvenation to ~age 20, added neural interface and cognitive implants, physical training.
  4. Final form: a custom combat exoskeleton "droid-carrier" housing the AI cluster, with his biological body kept as a backup inside a medical capsule in the suit.
- **Mind:** Bifurcates at activation — the defining transformation is cognitive, before any body change.
- **Pace:** Integration is surgical/sudden; body refinements come gradually after.
- **Reversibility:** Theoretically reversible but he refuses — psychologically permanent.
- **Distinctive:** Mind-transformation *precedes* and outweighs the body; the "body" ends as a wearable machine with the flesh as backup.

## 17-23. "Transformation Vignettes" (author: Michael Bard) — anthology of short, unusual TFs
URL: https://shifti.org/wiki/User:Michael_Bard/Transformation_Vignettes

Seven distinct concrete vignettes on one page:

- **17. Man -> toaster ("Not Awake Enough").** Trigger: chanting at his broken toaster; a force answers. Order: body locks up -> clothes vanish -> legs drop off -> arm detaches -> torso crushes into a rectangular cube -> skin turns chrome -> internals convert (tongue and teeth stretch/curl into heating filaments and elements, arm becomes plastic-and-wire). Mind: trapped, aware, mute. Pace: rapid discrete steps. Permanent — ends a working appliance.

- **18. Man + horse -> hippogriff ("Silent Record").** Trigger: shared mortal terror during a cliff fall; a force answers the will to survive. Order: the two bodies merge mid-fall -> legs shrink and scale over -> arms extend into wing structures -> head merges into an equine/avian hybrid -> chest barrels out around a new bone keel -> hind legs compress and settle either side of a re-formed sheath -> takes flight. Mind: human awareness fuses with the horse's into one. Pace: during freefall. Reads permanent. Distinctive: two beings combined into one flying creature.

- **19. Human teen -> skunk kit ("Wereing on the Moon").** Trigger: a specific light frequency from lunar silicon at sunrise activates a dormant were-condition seeded by an infected girlfriend's bite. Order: tail pushes through the clothes -> shrinking starts -> fur erupts everywhere -> feet reshape -> arms shorten and reorient -> head restructures, all while continuously shrinking. Mind: panic and animal instinct swamp the human mind; no memory of it after. Pace: multi-phase during light exposure. Reversible — reverts once sunrise passes. Distinctive: were-trigger is a moonlight *frequency* off lunar silicon, and it shrinks him to a juvenile.

- **20. Woman -> giant venomous snake ("Cold Vengeance").** Trigger: ritual blood-drinking plus an ancestral invocation, powered by hatred/vengeance. Order: hair falls out -> legs shrink and retract into the body -> a tail-bone erupts and grows muscle/vein/nerve -> scales push through and shove off dead skin in bloody shreds -> head narrows -> venom sacs swell in the cheeks -> eyes go yellow -> tongue splits. Mind: keeps human awareness at first, then merges with ancient reptilian rage. Pace: fairly rapid, vividly sensory. Reads permanent.

- **21. Woman -> theropod dinosaur ("Echoes of Extinction").** Trigger: ritual blood-drinking + invocation; possession by millions of extinct souls. Order: body collapses in a glow -> merged spirits fight for control -> scales burst through splitting skin (amber -> green/brown patterns) -> bones grow and warp -> head extends forward -> teeth reshape -> a counterbalancing tail grows -> a blood-filled crest rises along the spine. Mind: human consciousness overwhelmed and absorbed into the merged ancient spirits. Pace: gradual through an energy/glow state. Permanent; wakes hungry.

- **22. Planet Earth -> humanlike/godlike being ("Gaia").** Trigger: a planned 1000-year technological/spiritual convergence. Order: planetary restructuring -> planetary consciousness transferred into a prepared pod body -> ultimately reshaped via creation of a new universe. Mind: a planet-scale consciousness collapses into an individual one; madness precedes the transfer. Pace: millions of years of prep, instantaneous final transfer. Irreversible; the planet is destroyed. Distinctive: the "body" that transforms is an entire planet.

- **23. Abused child -> 1931 Rolls-Royce ("Otters, Otters Everywhere").** Trigger: benevolent intervention by two transcendent beings. Order: clothes vanish -> skin disperses into sparkling particles -> muscles untie and orbit the skeleton -> collapses into a red sphere -> sphere expands and elongates -> becomes a rectangular metal frame -> settles into the car. Mind: human awareness passes into the machine, described as contentment / absence of pain. Pace: rapid. Framed as permanent liberation. Distinctive: transformation as rescue — the car-body is relief from abuse.
